An Empirical Study On Voice And Animation In Visualization

Training Session

· For each trial, you will be shown a visualization image for 30 seconds.

· This is followed by a grey screen for 9 seconds.

· You will then be asked three questions about the visualization.

· Each question is a multiple choice with four optional answers.

· Your task is to choose the most appropriate answer based on the visualization that you just saw some 9 seconds ago.

· You have to rely on your memory to answer the question and will not be able to go back to see the visualization again.

· You should try to answer the question quickly and accurately.

· There is no time limit in answering the question though the time is recorded.

· After you have answered each question, click on the 'Next' button to continue.


When you are ready, click on the 'Start Training' button to training trial.
