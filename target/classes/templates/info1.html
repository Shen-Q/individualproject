What is the purpose of the study?

The goal of the study is to understand the effect of voice and animation in visualization.

Why have I been invited to take part?

This is an anonymous questionnaire. You can choose to participate voluntarily if you are over 18 years old and can use a computer. You must have normal vision, hearing and comprehension. We invite you to take part in this research study because you have showed an interest in taking part in our study.

What will happen if I take part?

After filling in the demographic questionnaire, you will begin the training for the study, where you will be shown a series visualization. Each visualization will be shown for 30 seconds after which you will be given three multiple-choice questions regarding each of the visualizations. Some of the visualization may contain charts with voice-over while some will contain only voice recording. During the 30 seconds that the visualization is shown, we asked that you observe and listen carefully to the chart and any voice recording about the visualization. Once you have completed the training, you will be directed to the main part of the study. In the main study, you will be asked to complete similar tasks that you performed in the training session.
